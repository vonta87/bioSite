Interview

  1. Why did you join the military? Because I wanted financial stability and wanted to move out of my hometown and travel.
  2. How was your job in the military? Physically and mentally challenging, only positive was the deployments I was tasked on to see other parts of the world.
  3. What did you do before the military? I was a teacher. I taught pre-school and first graders.
  4. How did you meet your husband? Actually, we met in training for our jobs. It is where we went after basic training.
  5. How was Greece for a deployment? It was the first time in my life where I left the country and was happy to learn the culture and getting to know the island. It was a learning experience from everything just a different culture to experience was nice.
  6. Do you miss the Air Force? No, my decision to separate was because I was ready for a new chapter in my life. I will always be grateful for the Air Force.
  7. Why did you separate from the Air Force? I wanted to dedicate my time to my family and being in the military was not going to allow me to be available the way I wanted to for my kids and future kids.
  8. Are you happy to be back in your hometown? Yes, I am happy to be near my family again and near the beach. Also, there Mexican food here beats all the Mexican food in America.
  9. How old is your son? 13 months
  10. How did your family feel knowing you were coming back? Beyond happy. They had their daughter back and their only grandson home.
